About Us

Welcome to Arial Security & Escort Services Pvt Ltd, a trusted name in the Labour Contractor industry. With 20 years of experience in the industry, we are committed to providing top-notch skilled & Unskilled workers, securityand labours services to our clients.

With a dedicated team of professionals, we strive to ensure the safety and satisfaction of our clients. We understand the importance of reliable and trustworthy security services, and that's why we go above and beyond to exceed expectations.

Key Features

Temporary Manpower Solutions:

Our engagements are purely temporary and based on contractual terms, relieving employers from long-term labour management responsibilities.

Comprehensive Benefits:

We ensure that outsourced personnel receive benefits like EPF, ESI, bonus, gratuity, incentives/perks, and increments as per labour rules and terms of agreement.

Efficient Payroll Management:

We handle payroll on behalf of employers, ensuring smooth salary disbursement and relieving them from labour-related administrative tasks.

Wide Range of Services:

We provide skilled/semi-skilled manpower, IT and engineering personnel, housekeeping services, daily wage laborers, office staff, data entry operators, security personnel, and survey agents.
